What is Bitcoin?

Bitcoin is a digital asset and a payment system. It is commonly called a decentralized digital currency. Transactions are verified by network nodes and recorded in a public distributed ledger called the blockchain.

What is Ethereum?

Ethereum is a world computer running on nodes distributed worldwide. It runs on a blockchain secured by miners executing a Proof of Work consensus algorithm, with plans to move to a Proof of Stake consensus algorithm.
